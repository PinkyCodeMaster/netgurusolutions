## Welcome to Athena

Athena is an all-in-one platform for all your eCommerce needs, developed as a monorepo solution for Net Guru Solutions. With Athena, we provide seamless support for eCommerce websites, backend logic, payments, shopping, email integration, and more. This platform will empower businesses to manage their online presence, backend, and admin systems all in one place.

### Components of Athena

- **Home Page (app)**: The central interface for Net Guru Solutions showcasing Athena’s capabilities, including hosting and eCommerce support.
- **API Server (api)**: Handles the backend eCommerce logic, including payment processing and shopping cart functionality.
- **Email Integration (email)**: Seamless email communications for order confirmations, receipts, and other notifications.
- **Admin Backend (web)**: A dedicated administrative interface for managing Athena's eCommerce operations and settings.
- **Documentation (doc)**: Comprehensive documentation to guide you through the setup, usage, and customization of Athena.
